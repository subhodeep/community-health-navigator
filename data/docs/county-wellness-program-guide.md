# Franklin Ridge County Wellness Program — 2026 Guide

## What the program covers
The County Wellness Program provides free preventive care to eligible residents:
annual checkups, blood-pressure and diabetes screenings, flu shots, nutrition
counseling, and up to six mental-health counseling sessions per year.

## Who is eligible
You qualify if **any** of the following applies:
- You are enrolled in **Medicaid** (any plan). Enrollment is automatic — bring your
  Medicaid card to any participating clinic.
- Your household income is at or below **200% of the federal poverty level**
  (sliding-scale fees apply between 150% and 200%).
- You are **uninsured** and a resident of Franklin Ridge County.
- You are **65 or older**, regardless of income.

Proof of residency (utility bill or lease) is required at first visit. No
immigration status questions are asked at any participating site.

## How to enroll
1. Find a participating clinic (all "free" and "low" cost-tier clinics participate).
2. Bring ID, proof of residency, and — if you have it — your Medicaid card or a
   recent pay stub.
3. Enrollment takes about 20 minutes and coverage starts the same day.

## Cost
- Medicaid enrollees and residents 65+: **no cost**.
- Sliding scale: $5–$25 per visit depending on household income.
- No one is turned away for inability to pay.

Questions: call the County Health Line at 555-0142 (Mon–Fri 8am–6pm, language
support available).
