# Free Flu Shot Clinics — Fall 2026 Schedule

The Community Flu Shot Drive (program P03) offers **free flu vaccinations — no
insurance, appointment, or ID required.**

## Walk-in schedule
| District | Location | Dates | Hours |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | D1 Wellness Center | Every Saturday, Sep 12 – Nov 21 | 9am – 1pm |
| D2 | Riverside Clinic | Tuesdays & Thursdays, Sep 15 – Nov 19 | 3pm – 7pm |
| D3 | Unity Community Center | Every Saturday, Sep 12 – Nov 21 | 9am – 1pm |
| D4 | D4 Family Health Clinic | Wednesdays, Sep 16 – Nov 18 | 10am – 4pm |
| D5 | Franklin Ridge General (lobby) | Mon–Fri, Oct 1 – Oct 30 | 8am – 6pm |
| D6 | D6 Wellness Center | Every Sunday, Sep 13 – Nov 22 | 10am – 2pm |

## Good to know
- Children 6 months and older can be vaccinated; under-18s need a guardian present.
- High-dose vaccine for residents 65+ is available at all hospital sites and the
  D1 and D6 wellness centers.
- Pharmacies with the `flu_shot` service also give free flu shots year-round to
  Wellness Program members.
- If you had a severe reaction to a previous flu vaccine, talk to a clinician
  first — hospital sites have one on duty.

Language interpreters are available on-site at the D2 and D3 locations.
