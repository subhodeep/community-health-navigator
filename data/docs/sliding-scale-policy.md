# Sliding-Scale Fee Policy — Participating Facilities

Facilities marked `sliding_scale` in the county registry charge according to
household income, verified once per year.

## Fee schedule (per primary-care or counseling visit)
| Household income (% of federal poverty level) | Fee |
|---|---|
| At or below 150% | $0 |
| 151% – 200% | $5 – $15 |
| 201% – 250% | $16 – $25 |
| Above 250% | Standard rates apply |

Screenings, immunizations, and Wellness Program services are **always free** at
participating sites regardless of income.

## Verification
Bring **one** of: most recent tax return, two recent pay stubs, or a benefits
award letter (SNAP, TANF, SSI — any of these automatically qualifies you for the
$0 tier). Self-declaration is accepted for a first visit if documents are not
available; verification is completed within 60 days.

## Emergency care
Sliding-scale rules do not apply to hospital emergency departments. Under county
charity-care rules, emergency care at Franklin Ridge General and St. Amara is
billed on a separate hardship schedule — ask for a financial counselor within
30 days of your visit.

## Appeals
If you believe your fee tier is wrong, call the County Health Line (555-0142) or
ask any front desk for a Fee Review form. Reviews are decided within 10 business
days and fees are frozen while a review is open.
